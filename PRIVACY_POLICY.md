# Privacy Policy for RealEnglish

**Last Updated:** May 30, 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at RealEnglish. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

## 1. Information We Collect
**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.**
RealEnglish is a standalone game designed to help you learn English. You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or share any identifying data to use the app.

## 2. Local Data Storage
All of your game progress, including your XP, levels, daily streaks, and custom word libraries, is stored **locally on your device**. We do not have servers that sync or backup this data. If you uninstall the app, your progress will be deleted from your device.

## 3. Optional AI Integration (Ollama)
The app includes an optional feature that allows you to generate custom crosswords using a local AI model via **Ollama**. This feature requires you to run an Ollama server on your own computer or local network. The app communicates directly with the server address you provide — no data is sent to us or any third party. This feature is entirely optional; the app works fully offline without it.

## 4. Network Access
The app requests local network access **only** when you enable the optional Ollama AI feature to connect to a server you configure on your local network. No data is transmitted to external servers by us.

## 5. Children's Privacy
Our application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
